About Me

I'm Sabina, a passionate UX/UI Designer dedicated to crafting digital experiences that are both beautiful and functional. With a minimalist approach, I design interfaces that are clean, intuitive, and engaging.

My design philosophy is all about putting users first, while maintaining a strong sense of visual harmony. I believe that great design should feel effortless—natural to use, yet powerful in impact.
